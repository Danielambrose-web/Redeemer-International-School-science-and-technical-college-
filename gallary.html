Explore memorable moments from Redeemer International Schools Science and Technical College.

UCMAS Class

Date: 26/09/2022
As our school resumed for the 2022/2023 session, we introduced our students to Universal Concept of Mental Arithmetic System training (UCMAS). UCMAS builds critical thinking skills, improves focus, boosts mathematical accuracy, and increases confidence in students.

Admission forms are available at Redeemer Intl Schools (Science and Technical College). Address: No. 8 Udotung Ubo Lane, Uyo.

Robotics Exhibition at Ibom Icon

Date: 01/08/2022
On July 7th, 2022, our students attended the UYOCCIMA lecture event, where they showcased creativity across ICT, Woodwork, Arts, and Science.

Exhibited items included: Art portraits, wooden/Ankara picture frames, beaded crafts, traditional hats, disinfectants (RISSTC IZAL), serving carts, drawing boards, lamps, WiFi prototypes, and our AI-Robot kit.

Venue: Ibom Icon Hotel.

Girl Child Day

Date: 11/10/2022
Theme: "The Time is Now: Our Right, Our Future."
Some of our students celebrated with the Women in Total Girl Development (Wit-Girl) group. They participated in symposiums and discussions.

“Happy Girlhood, Happy Motherhood!”

Admission forms are available at Redeemer International Schools: Science and Technical College, No. 8 Udotung Ubo Lane, Uyo.

Science and Technology Week

Date: 27/04/2022
Redeemer International Schools hosted its Science and Technology Week.

Topic: Life Skills and Workforce (Project-Based Learning).
Students learned teamwork, critical thinking, and problem-solving. They also used Google Jamboard to sketch ideas.

Highlights: Group projects, collaboration, and presentations.
