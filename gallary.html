(UCMAS) class

26/09/2022 As our school resumes for the 2022/2023 session, we introduced our students to Universal Concept of Mental Aritmetic System training (UCMAS). Ucmas is a training aimed at building student's critical thinking skills, improves learning concentration and focus, it boost mathematical accuracy and confidence in students. Admission forms are available at Redeemer Int'l Schools (Science and technical) college. Address: No. 8 Udotung ubo lane , Uyo.

Robotics Exhibition at Ibom Icon

01/08/2022 On the 7th of July 2022, our students were privileged to attend the UYOCCIMA lecture event that hosted creative minds who exhibited their handcrafts and products. Our school exhibited products from four major department in the school; ICT, Woodwork, Arts and Science department. Products exhibited were: Art design portrait, Wooden/Ankara designed picture frame, Hand/Neck beads and Beaded Key-holder, Traditional design hats, Ankara design book cover, Toilet disinfectant (RISSTC IZAL), Wooden Serving Cart/tray, Technical drawing board, Reading Lamp(Made from locally sourced material), Student's WiFi Ongoing Prototype Project and our already coded AI-Robot kit was also on display. All of these showcased our school as an institution that aims at training students to become self-reliant and problem solvers in the world. VENUE OF EVENT: IBOM ICON HOTEL
