About Us | Our Story

Redeemer International Schools: Science And Technical College (RISSTC) is a Government-approved Christian School founded in 2015. The school is located in a safe and serene environment at the heart of the town. RISSTC is committed to catching them young to be well rooted in Christ for the marketplace, especially in the area of Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM).

Redeemer International Schools comprises one non-profit early childhood education, as well as a Science and Technical College.

We are working on getting the Early Childhood Education Center registered with the Ministry of Education in Akwa Ibom State as a recognized center for primary education.

Our Science and Technical College is located in Uyo. It is designed to promote science and technical education in the state, while empowering our youths to be entrepreneurial and successful in the global market. We run a Junior College program that covers the full curriculum for science, arts, and business courses.

WELCOME TO REDEEMER INTERNATIONAL SCHOOLS:
- Early Childhood Education Center
- Science and Technical College

In the Senior Secondary College, we run two parallel streams: the Senior Science College and the Senior Technical College. Students also have the choice to continue to the one-year Advanced Technical or Science College program after completing the senior secondary college.

The Early Childhood Education Center is located in Ikot Akpamba, Nsit Ubium LGA. It is a project of Peace-Link for Education and Rural Development, an NGO headed by Mrs Comfort Ekaette. This center was born out of the burden to see young children receive good and functional education that supports their dreams, goals, and ambitions.

Science and Technical College Streams

Redeemer International Schools: Science and Technical College (RISSTC) runs five college streams:

- The Junior College
- The Senior Science College
- The Senior Technical College
- The Advanced Technical College
- The Advanced Science College

Junior College

The Junior Secondary School (JSS) runs for three years for primary school leavers. Students study subjects such as: Mathematics, English Language & Literature, Basic Science & Technology, Business Studies, Religion & National Values, Cultural & Creative Arts, Pre-vocational Studies, one Nigerian Language, French, and Entrepreneurship.

In their third year, students write:
- Junior School Certificate Examination (Akwa Ibom State Ministry of Education)
- Basic Education Certificate Examination (NECO)

Senior Science College

Subjects offered: Mathematics, English Language, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Agricultural Science, Geography, Further Mathematics, Computer & ICT, English Literature, Economics, Technical Drawing.

Exams taken:
- Senior Secondary Certificate Examination (SSCE - WAEC)
- Senior School Certificate Examination (SSCE - NECO)

Senior Technical College

Departments:
1. Engineering Trades: Electrical Installation, Radio/TV & Electronics Work, Computer Craft Studies
2. Construction Trades: Block Laying, Carpentry, Air-conditioning, Refrigeration, Welding & Fabrication, Painting & Decorating
3. Miscellaneous Trades: Garment Making, Catering Craft Practice
4. Business Trades: Secretarial Studies, Bookkeeping (Accounting)
5. Trade Related Courses: Building/Engineering Drawing, Basic Electricity, General Metal Work, General Wood

Certification: Modular Certificate, National Technical Certificate (NTC), National Business Certificate (NBC) by NABTEB.

Advanced Programs

- Advanced Technical College: For holders of NTC/NBC, leading to Advanced Craft Certification (ANTC/ANBC) by NABTEB.
- Advanced Science College: For graduates of the Senior Science College, leading to A-Level examinations.
