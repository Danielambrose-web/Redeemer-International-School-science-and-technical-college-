ABOUT US| OUR STORY

Redeemer International Schools: Science And Technical College (RISSTC) is a Government approved Christian School founded in 2015. The school is located in a safe and serene environment at the heart of the town. RISSTC is committed to catching them young to be well rooted in Christ for the marketplace especially in the area of Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM).
Redeemer International Schools comprises one non-profit early childhood education, as well as, a science and technical college.
We are working on getting the Early Childhood Education Center registered with the Ministry of Education in Akwa Ibom State as a recognized center for primary education.
Our Science and Technical College is located in Uyo. It is designed to promote science and technical education in the state, while empowering our youths to be entrepreneurial, and successful in the global market. We run a junior college program that covers the full curriculum for both science, arts and business courses.

The college runs a three-year Junior college for primary school leavers - the Junior Secondary School (JSS). Students are offered studies in the following subject areas : Mathematics, English language and literature, Basic science and technology, Business studies, Religion and national values, Cultural and creative arts, Pre-vocational studies, One Nigerian language; French and Entrepreneurship. In their third year the students are administered two examinations:
The Junior School Certificate Examination conducted by the Akwa Ibom State Ministry of Education
Basic Education Certificate Examination conducted by National Examinations Council (NECO)
For the senior college - the Senior Secondary School (SSS), the school runs two parallel streams: A three year Senior Science College and a three year Senior Technical College. Graduates of the JSS program may continue their studies in either of these streams following an in-house evaluation of their strengths and interests.
The Senior Science College offers the following subjects: Mathematics, English Language, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Agricultural Science, Geography, Further mathematics, Computer and information technology, English Literature, Economics and Technical Drawing. Students in this stream will be administered the following examinations:
Senior Secondary Certificate Examination (SSCE) conducted by West African Examination Council (WAEC)
Senior School Certificate Examination (SSCE) conducted by National Examination Council (NECO)

The Senior Technical College shall offer artisan and craft level programs for the award of Modular Certificate, National Technical Certificate (NTC) and National Business Certificate (NBC) by NABTEB.
The Advanced Technical College runs for holders of NTC and NBC or equivalent qualifications . The advanced technical college offers a program leading to Advanced Craft Certification (ANTC and ANBC) by NABTEB.
The Advanced Science College runs for graduates of the Senior Science College, and leads to the administration of A-Level examinations.
