about us

Redeemer International Schools: Science And Technical College (RISSTC) is a Government approved Christian School founded in 2015. The school is located in a safe and serene environment at the heart of the town. RISSTC is committed to catching them young to be well rooted in Christ for the marketplace especially in the area of Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM). Redeemer International Schools comprises one non-profit early childhood education, as well as, a science and technical college. We are working on getting the Early Childhood Education Center registered with the Ministry of Education in Akwa Ibom State as a recognized center for primary education. Our Science and Technical College is located in Uyo. It is designed to promote science and technical education in the state, while empowering our youths to be entrepreneurial, and successful in the global market. We run a junior college program that covers the full curriculum for both science, arts and business courses.
